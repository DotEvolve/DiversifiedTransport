We have over 25 years experience

Diversified Transport Limited is a Third Party Logistics (TPL) to project contractors in Africa. With our base in Tanzania, we have been working on the custom clearance, transport, and project management supply chain solutions for some of the largest infrastructure projects in the region. We have an integrated supply chain and logistics solutions that are unmatched in East Africa.

Our status as a diversified, premier, TPL provider is dependant upon our ability to provide only the highest level of service to our customers, and to recognise the opportunities that are available in the East African market.
