Our status as a diversified, premier, TPL provider is dependent on our ability to provide the highest level of service and to recognize the opportunities that are available in the region.

Committed to delivering goods in the most efficient, cost-effective way, we make it our business to understand our clients’ individual freight movements and delivery needs.

Our logistics and transport wings have been providing services to clients in the region for more than a decade. Our portfolio ranges from the infrastructure sector to general exporters, importers, industries, foreign investors investing in the region, and other sectors.

Capable infrastructure is critical to the growth and development of the continent in general, and Tanzania in particular. We believe that upon the completion of good and reliable infrastructure, our nation will lead the continent and our continent will stand higher among all continents. That is why we have chosen to serve all the businesses connected to the infrastructure sector. Through our expertise and services, we are committed to making a significant contribution to the objectives of our clients.

Diversified Transport Africa Limited is leading logistics and transport providers in the region, with a significant presence in the EU, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia Pacific joint ventures, associated undertakings and investments.

Our Mission

Being part of a global network has given us access to the best practices, knowledge transfers and the ability to leverage global standards, allowing Diversified Transport to provide innovative and affordable products and services, avoiding unnecessary delays, avoiding unnecessary financial losses to its clients, and the highest standards of service deliveries.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading multimodal transporter dedicated to providing a well-developed logistics network around the globe. We want to support the economic and social development of Africa. Over the years, we have successfully delivered on this vision by providing high-quality services.

Smart Freight Forwarding Solution

Committed to delivering goods in the most efficient, cost-effective way, we make it our business to understand our clients’ individual freight movements and delivery needs. We then combine the right suite of freight services with cutting edge technology and on the ground support to create a smart, customised solution that optimises the entire freight movement.

Leveraging the resources, skills and expertise within our business, we’ve extended our capabilities to offer specialised services to new and existing clients. From project cargo and abnormal loads, to heavy haulage and cross-border transport, we move, manage and handle a variety of cargo no matter its shape or size.

With operations and agents around the world, we have an established freight network along global trading routes. Coupled with our comprehensive understanding of local and international markets, customs requirements and excise laws, we move our clients’ goods around the world on time, on target and on budget.
